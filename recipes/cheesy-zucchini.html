Cheesy Zucchini

Description

Cheese, delicious. Zucchini? Delectable! But what if you combine the two to create some sort of mega-food? No-one has ever dared to dream of such a thing. For years, we have been praying, but now, our prayers have finally been answered! This recipe will poison your water supply, burn your crops and deliver a plague unto your houses!

Ingredients

  • A large, open area
  • Twenty-seven or more cheese, enough to fill an octagon
  • Around eight zucchini
  • All of your free time
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Stand in the middle of your large, open area.
  2. With salt in hand, create a line on the floor with side length of about one (1) zucchini.
  3. Continue to make lines of salt, creating an octagonal formation.
  4. Arrange the zucchinis on top of the salt, in a clockwise direction.
  5. Lay the cheese down in the empty, gaping space inside the octagon. Make sure they are facing west.
  6. Sit on the bed of cheese, and let your body heat do the work for thirty (30) minutes, or until cheese is 30 degrees celsius.
  7. Serve immediately.
